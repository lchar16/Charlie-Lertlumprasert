About Me

Hi! My name is Charlie Lertlumprasert. I was born in Thailand, and moved to Northern Virginia when I was 9 years old. Student Engineers' Council

The Student Engineers' Council (SEC) at Virginia Tech is an umbrella organization devoted to serving the University's College of Engineering. We help foster a professional network for students by organizing events such as E-Week, Engineering Exposition, and the Leadership in Engineering Conference. We grant over $160,000 to design teams, engineering organizations, and faculty. Ultimately, our mission is to provide students with the tools and opportunities they need to succeed, both in and out of the classroom.

For our National Conference, I edited this video to submit as part of our Best Overall Large Council application. My goal was to summarize all the work that we do.
